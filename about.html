Coldplay is a British rock band who formed in 1996, when 4 university friends came together.
Originally named Pectoralz and then Starfish, Chris Martin, Jonny Buckland, Guy Berryman and Will Champion eventually decided on the name Coldplay.
With their first hit album Yellow (released 2000) providing them with worldwide fame, their futures were set in the music industry.
Having won 107 awards from 252 nominations, Coldplay remain one of the most successful bands to have been formed.

Born in Exeter on the 2nd of March 1977, Chris Martin was the oldest of 5.
His mother was a music teacher, so music was one of his passions from a young age.
In 1996, he attended University College London, where he met Jonny Buckland.
This was the start of one of the worlds greatest bands. They were called Pectoralz. Soon to be Coldplay.
Since then he has toured most of the world, not only with Coldplay, but also with solo work.

Jonny Buckland was born in Islington on the 11th September 1977.
At the age of 11, he started playing guitar. Some of his inspirations were The Stone Roses and Ride.
His favourite guitar to use and current guitar of choice is a Telecaster.
He went to University College London, where he intended to study astronomy and mathematics.
This is where he met Chris Martin, together forming Coldplay.

Guy Berryman was born in Kirkcaldy on April 12th 1978.
He attended University College London to study Engineering.
After one year, he dropped out of university to focus on playing the bass for Coldplay.
As well as his work with Coldplay, Guy has had many successful musical adventures by himself and with others.
He has also been part of the photography crew for the band, taking many pictures for the Twisted Logic and X&Y Tours.

Born in Southampton on July 31st 1978, Will Champion originally learnt to play the piano and violin.
By age 12, Will Champion had also learnt to play the guitar, bass and tin whistle.
In 1997, he was the last to join Coldplay, becoming their drummer.
He has also completed many other projects on his own and with others, including an appearance on Game of Thrones.
